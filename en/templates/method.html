Method

(1) Measured room temparature t1[℃]

(2) Stood the tube on the stand like this picture, and made the water surface approach to the open side of the tube.

(3) Moved the tuning fork away from the open side of the tube,tapped it lightly with a tapping stick, and moved to the open side of the tube quickly.

Then, moved the water surface down slowly by moving the water reservoir.

(4) Found out the position of the water surface where the air column resonates with the tuning fork and is heard the loudest.

(5) Measured the distance L1 from the tube opening to the water surface.

(6)From the first resonance point, found the point which the air column resonates with the turning fork again, and measured the distance L2 from the tube`s open to the water surface.

(7) Repeated steps (2)to(6)three times.

(8) Finally, measured room temperature t2(℃).
